NSBM MUSIC CLUB

Music club of NSBM provides opportunities for students to showcase their talents in broad range of music activities. Students of similar passion for music come together and help each other learn and develop their skills in this field. Every year, members of the club get the chance to compose NSBM Siyapath Siya Udanaya –Aurudu Theme song, Poson Song etc. which promotes NSBM brand name. Members also get the chance to organize and perform in various Musical Shows to showcase their talents. Encore 2017, Haritha Iswara 2018 and Musical Show in 2019 to kick start the Opendays of NSBM are few examples of Musical events organized by Music Club of NSBM. They also perform in Religious Musical events like Poson Bakthi Gee and provide entertainment at other events organized by NSBM. Currently, it is guided by music loving Senior Lecturer – Mr. Dileepa Jayamanne (Master in charge) and dynamic Lecturer Ms. Ishani Karunaratna (Mistress in charge)

NSBM HEALTH & WELLNESS CLUB (WELLBEING ASSOCIATION)

This Association was initiated at the beginning of the year 2018, with the intention of advocating the mental, physical, social and spiritual wellbeing among the undergraduates of Green University Town, as well as to pave a path towards the betterment of the entire Sri Lankan nation in terms of ultimate features of wellbeing. The first and only student wellbeing association initiated in Sri Lankan University and expects to promote wellbeing in molding its own path for the betterment of younger generation. NSBM Student Wellbeing Association wishes to be the first and foremost in overall aspects.
